The Challenge We're Solving

African smallholder farmers face critical barriers to productivity and sustainability

💪

Manual Labor & Inefficiency

Farmers rely on outdated tools like hoes and cutlasses, leading to fatigue, low yields, and limited scalability.

🌍

Environmental Damage

Heavy reliance on herbicides and diesel-powered tools harms soil fertility and contributes to greenhouse gas emissions.

💧

Water Scarcity

Inefficient irrigation systems waste precious resources that communities share for both irrigation and domestic use.

📦

Import Dependence

Imported machinery is expensive, ill-suited to local soil, and difficult to maintain, reducing local innovation.

Our Solutions

Locally engineered, AI-powered tools designed for African farming realities

🔋

Battery-Powered Sprayer

Replaces manual pumping with rechargeable, automated spraying. Lightweight and ergonomic to reduce strain, especially for women farmers.

🌱

Hand-Held Weeder

Electric-powered, fast, and safe alternative to manual weeding. Reduces chemical herbicide usage and labor time by over 70%.

💨

Mist Blower

Battery-operated, replacing fuel-powered models. Zero carbon emissions, quiet, and durable for modern farming needs.

⚙️

Multi-Functional Soil Tool

Multi-purpose design for soil preparation, replacing the hoe and plow. Minimizes farmer fatigue and improves soil structure.

🤖

AI Laser Weeding Machine

Detects weeds vs. crops using AI vision. Uses lasers or mechanical blades to remove weeds without herbicides, protecting soil health.

💧

Smart Irrigation System

IoT-based sensors monitor soil moisture and climate. Automatically irrigates when needed, saving up to 35% of water. Solar-powered for off-grid use.
